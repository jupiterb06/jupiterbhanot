    about

    jupiter bhanot (they/them) is a 19 year old artist currently studying fine art at cornell university’s college of architecture, art, and planning. they work with a variety of media, including (but not limited to): drawing, painting, graphic design, video, game design, and web development. their work explores themes of fluidity, queerness, the body, digitality (as well as post-digitality), and flux states. while primarily representational, it draws heavy inspiration from conceptual methods and techniques.

    outside of the visual arts, jupiter is currently the editorial director for thread magazine, a publication based out of cornell university, and writes for both thread and their own personal blog.

    jupiter is always open to commissions, collaborations, and freelance work. please feel free to reach out, either via the contact page, or via emailing jupiterbhanot@gmail.com.
